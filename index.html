PROJECT
DISINFECTANT ROBOT

ROLES and RESPONSIBILITY

I am one of project associate(Hardware Connection) in a team of the 4 members to deign a robot to sanitize the operation room in hospital, college and industries so that every individual are out of risk in the pandemic.

DESCRIPTION

The source of the infections in operating or patient rooms in hospitals can be difficult to identify, as patients, visitors, and physical objects can be transfer agents. Therefore, a comprehensive disinfection is necessary to minimize the risk of transmission and infection. If sanitization is done by human there is possibility that a person might also get infected. Hence an Ultraviolet(UV) sterilizationis used to aid in reduction of micro-organisms that may remain on the surface after a standard cleaning to the minimum number. Disinfectant Robot is used to santize an operating or a patient room and has UV lamps mounted on the top of the robot platform covering 360 degree direction. The disinfectant robot is employed by an Embedded System based on Arduino Uno to aid in navigation to avoid obstacles.
